## How to Use

### 1. Pick a Video
Tap the **"Pick Video"** button on the home screen to select a video from your device.

### 2. Play Video
- Video starts playing automatically
- Tap screen to show/hide controls
- Swipe left for brightness control
- Swipe right for volume control
- Double-tap left to rewind 10s
- Double-tap right to forward 10s

### 3. Fullscreen Mode
Tap the fullscreen button (â›¶) to rotate to landscape mode. The device will automatically rotate!

### 4. View History
Your recently played videos appear on the home screen with:
- Thumbnail preview
- File size and duration
- Last played time
- Watch progress bar
- Play count badge

### 5. Swipe Actions
Swipe left on any video card to:
- **Share** - Share the video (coming soon)
- **Delete** - Remove from history

### 6. Statistics
Tap the statistics icon (ğŸ“Š) to view:
- Total videos watched
- Total play count
- Total watch time
- Average plays per video
- Most played videos

## Troubleshooting

### Video Not Playing
- Check if the file still exists
- Try picking the video again
- Check file format is supported

### Rotation Not Working
- Check device rotation lock is OFF
- Try tapping fullscreen button again
- Restart the app if needed

### Thumbnails Not Showing
- Thumbnails generate on first play
- May take a few seconds
- Check storage permissions

### History Not Saving
- Check app permissions
- Ensure sufficient storage
- Try clearing app cache
